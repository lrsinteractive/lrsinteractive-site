Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Overview

Replay: Save & Watch Later is operated by LRS Interactive LLC. Replay helps users save and organize links, videos, and posts shared from other apps.

Information Collection and Use

Replay can be used without creating an account. When used without signing in, saved links and collections are stored locally on the user's device.

Replay also offers optional sign-in features, such as cloud sync and shared collections. If a user chooses to sign in, Replay may use third-party services to authenticate the user and sync app data associated with that account.

Optional Account and Sync Features

Replay may use services such as Apple Sign In, Google Sign-In, Firebase Authentication, and Firebase Firestore to provide optional account, sync, and collection-sharing features. These services may process information such as account identifiers, email addresses or private relay email addresses, and saved app data needed to provide those features.

Saved Content

Replay stores links and related information that users choose to save in the app. If cloud sync or sharing is enabled, saved links and collection information may be synced using third-party cloud services so that the user's content can be accessed across devices or shared with invited users.

Advertisements

Replay may use third-party advertising services to display advertisements. These services may collect limited device and usage information for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

Replay may use third-party services for authentication, cloud storage, app functionality, analytics, crash reporting, advertisements, and related services. These third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Account Deletion

Users who create an account can request account deletion from within the app, where available, or by contacting Replay support. Deleting an account may remove account-associated cloud data unless retention is required for legal, security, or operational reasons.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Replay support at support@lrsinteractive.co.
